Privacy Policy for Rezeptmomente / Recipe Moments

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Rezeptmomente is a recipe organization app that helps users create, save, organize, and plan recipes, ingredients, instructions, photos, attachments, and shopping lists.

Developer contact:
Jakob Ritter
appdev.jr2024@gmail.com

1. Data We Collect or Process

Rezeptmomente may process the following data:

- Account information: email address and password/login information used to create or access an app account.
- User-created recipe content: recipe titles, categories, ingredients, instructions, serving information, shopping list items, notes, photos, and attachments added by the user.
- Camera and media data: photos or videos selected or captured by the user for recipes and attachments.
- Purchase information: premium purchase status handled through Google Play Billing.
- Device and technical data: basic technical information that may be processed by Android, Google Play, or third-party services used by the app.

2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide recipe creation and organization features.
- Save and display recipes, ingredients, instructions, photos, attachments, and shopping lists.
- Enable premium features through Google Play Billing.
- Improve app functionality, reliability, and user experience.
- Respond to support requests.

3. Data Sharing

Rezeptmomente does not sell personal data.

Data may be processed by third-party services required for app functionality, such as:

- Google Play Billing for premium purchases.
- Google Play services required for app distribution, purchases, and platform functionality.

We do not share user-created recipe content with other users or third parties unless the user chooses to export or share it.

4. Photos, Camera, and Attachments

The app may request camera or media access so users can add photos or videos to recipes. These files are used only for the recipe and attachment features selected by the user.

5. Data Storage and Security

Recipe data, photos, attachments, categories, and shopping list information are stored by the app for app functionality. We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

User-created content is kept as long as the user keeps it in the app. Users can delete recipes, categories, photos, attachments, and shopping list items inside the app.

If users want their account or related data deleted, they can contact us at:

appdev.jr2024@gmail.com

7. Children

Rezeptmomente is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact
